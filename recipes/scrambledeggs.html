Scrambled eggs

Desription

Scrambled eggs are a great way to start the day. I generally feel better whenever I eat something warm for breakfast, and this is, I believe, the easiest meal to prepare in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • A bit of butter (vegetable oil also works)
  • Salt
  • Chives (optional)

Steps

  1. Warm up the butter on a frying pan
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan
  3. Stir the eggs as they warm up
  4. Add a bit of salt, apparently the later, the better
  5. Add some chives if you want

And here you go. Obviously you can make scrambled eggs in many fancier ways, but that's the way I do it.
