Cauliflower dahl

Description

This is a recipe from one of my vegan friends. It's a very nutritious meal that can be done quickly with a bit of practice and multitasking skills.

Ingredients

  • Half a cauliflower
  • 1 cup of red lentils
  • 1 onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of curry spice
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 200 ml of coconut milk (1/2 can)

Steps

  1. Grate the cauliflower and put it in a separate bowl.
  2. Cook the lentils in a large pot. You need one and a half cup of water for one cup of lentils. Cook them until they absorb the water.
  3. While the lentils are cooking, dice the onion and warm it up on a pan.
  4. Put the grated cauliflower on the pan, together with the spices and stir.
  5. When everything is done, put the cauliflower in the pot with lentils.
  6. Add the coconut milk and mix everything together.

And here you go. This is the most complicated recipe out of the ones I posted. It is not really possible to mess it up, though. At worst, the kitchen might look a little messy after you're done.
