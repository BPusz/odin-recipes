Fried rice

Description

Fried rice was one of the first meals I learned to prepare that I was happy with. It's quick, filling, and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of cooked, cold rice
  • Vegetables of choice: I use carrots, onions, broccoli and green peas, but you can use whatever you want
  • 1 egg
  • Butter
  • 1-2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce

Steps

  1. Scramble your egg and put it aside
  2. Slice and dice your vegetables
  3. Warm up your vegetables on a frying pan
  4. Add the rice and sauces and stir
  5. Add the scrambled eggs at the end and stir a bit more

And here you go. The beauty of fried rice is that you modify it to satisfy your needs. You can experiment with different ingredients, add more/different seasoning, etc.
